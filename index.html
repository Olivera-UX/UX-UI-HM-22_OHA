What It Is

Bloom Time is a time table companion app for MS Teams that shows your team's day at a glance in real time. See what they are up to at any point in the day, so you know what time works for them without asking! It also features a Team Plant that will grow as your team is productive!

How It Works

Bloom Time will automatically sync with your Outlook calendar to seamlessly intergrate into our app. At any moment of the day, you can see what your team is up to, no need to ask!

Why You Want It

Bloom Time focuses on helping teams manage their days and reducing unnecessary stress. The ability to connect with your team at the right time is invaluable! Additionally, plants in the office are known to decrease stress. Bloom Time's garden will help your team to relax and grow.
